So Sharp Knife Sharpeners offers product specific training sessions for our produts! Each session covers how to use the specific product, information about when to use it such as for repairing, sharpening, polishing; what makes it different from other products, and allows hands-on instruction. Instructors are notable So Sharp employees who specialize in the type of sharpener including our very own David Hands!

There are also free drop-in general information sessions about our various types of knife sharpeners where you can come and go as you please.
