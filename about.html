About So Sharp

So Sharp Knife Sharpeners has always believed that your knives should be “so sharp” that your chopping, cutting, and slicing will not be limited by the blade you use, but rather, what you can imagine using them for. So Sharp Knife Sharpeners wishes to give our customers the freedom to cut through whatever their heart desires, which can only be accomplished with a sharp mind and sharper blade.

So Sharp Knife Sharpeners provide our customers with the solution to their cutting dreams with our knife sharpeners. Knives require regular maintenance to keep their blades in top cutting, slicing, and chopping condition. Our company’s knife sharpeners will keep your knives in the best possible condition and even give new life to your own dull blades. To accomplish this, we sell only the highest quality knife sharpeners at a value that our customers can appreciate. Our knife sharpeners make your knives “so sharp” you will not believe it is not a brand-new knife.

Products

So Sharp Knife Sharpeners sells three types of knife sharpeners: whetstone, electric, and manual. The sharpeners have various amounts of grit ranging from 160 to 8000. The manual pull-through style has the least grit at 160. The manual type allows the knife sharpener to be available at a lower price range than the electric models. Electric knife sharpeners will sharpen the knives without manual effort and have higher grit than manual type knife sharpeners. Finally, whetstones have the highest amount of grit available at the higher price point in order to maintain high quality blades. Whetstones varieties are: water stone, diamond stone, oil stone, and a flattening stone for the water stone.
